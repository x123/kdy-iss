# Welcome to the KDY International Sailing School 2026 Season

We continue to offer our well-known sailing school format on **Wednesday and
Thursday**, and we are also continuing with our fleet racing on **Fridays**.

**New for this year:** join us for 1-on-1 match racing on **Mondays**!

---

## Sailing School Modules (Wednesday and Thursday)

The sailing school is divided into three modules:

* **Beginner – Start Sailing**

    In this first module, which requires no prior sailing skills, you will get
    started with crewing on sailing boats. You will learn how to rig a boat,
    get underway, handle sails, steer, and assist with harbor maneuvers. By the
    end of the season, you will be able to crew on a keelboat in moderate
    sailing conditions.

    *During winter, we recommend you participate in the theory course for the
    Proficiency of Yachting, which will help you achieve your
    **Duelighedsbevis** by the end of the following season.*

* **Intermediate – Certificate of Proficiency in Yachting (DK: Duelighedsbevis)**

    During the second module, you will consolidate your crewing skills, develop
    helming skills, and build overall sailing awareness and basic skipper
    competences. By the end of the season, you will be able to crew, helm, and
    skipper a keelboat in moderate sailing conditions.

    *You will be prepared to take the practical sailing test and obtain the
    Certificate of Proficiency in Yachting, provided you have passed the
    Duelighed theory test.*

* **Advanced - KDY Skipper & Spinnaker Handling**

    During our final module, you will further improve your boat handling and
    skipper skills, as well as become familiar with spinnaker handling. At the
    end of the season, you will be able to take the KDY Skipper License, which
    allows you to skipper the club’s boats.

---

## Fleet Racing (Friday)

* **J/70 Fleet Race Training**

    For sailors who have completed the Intermediate module, we offer J/70 Fleet
    Race training on Fridays. The focus is on developing practical racing
    skills and improving performance on the racecourse. Training includes boat
    and rig setup, sail trim, and techniques to optimize boat speed. We will
    practice precise maneuvers, including mark roundings, starts, and gennaker
    handling. Sessions also introduce the Racing Rules of Sailing and cover key
    racing concepts such as starting tactics, positioning, and course strategy.

---

## Upcoming Match Race (Monday)

* **DS 37 Match Race Training**

    For those who have passed the Intermediate module, we offer DS 37 Match
    Race training. The main goal is to learn and improve your tactical racing
    skills in a one-on-one format. We will study boat handling, specific DS 37
    trim, and exercise tight maneuvers unique to match racing, such as dial-ups
    and circling. You will be introduced to the Racing Rules of Sailing and
    learn about pre-start duels and defensive course strategies.

## How to Enroll KDY

You must be an active member of the Royal Danish Yacht Club (KDY) before you
can sign up for the ISS sailing school.

### Enroll at KDY

1. **Apply for KDY membership here:**
   [https://kdy.dk/indmeldelse/](https://kdy.dk/indmeldelse/)
   * **PLEASE INDICATE YOU WANT TO JOIN ISS INTERNATIONAL SAILING SCHOOL**
2. Upon receipt of your application, you will be invoiced for the membership
   fee.
3. **See membership prices here:**
   [https://kdy.dk/bliv-medlem/medlemspriser/](https://kdy.dk/bliv-medlem/medlemspriser/)

### Prepare Sign Up for ISS Sailing

1. Download the new membership system app, **Onboard**, and create your sailor
   profile.
   * **iPhone:** [Onboard App - App
     Store](https://apps.apple.com/dk/app/onboard/id1500644023)
   * **Android:** [Onboard – Apps i Google
     Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.onboard.onboardapp)
2. **Crucial:** When creating your profile, use the same email address you
   used to sign up for KDY.

Once you have paid your membership fee, we will add you to the relevant
communities in the Onboard App enabling you to sign up.

Go to KDY, select **ISS International Sailing School** and choose your
preferred **Event**. In the Event you should also select the preferred sailing
level.

> **Note:** If you enroll in KDY and we cannot offer you your preferred weekday
> or sailing level, KDY will reimburse you your membership fee.
